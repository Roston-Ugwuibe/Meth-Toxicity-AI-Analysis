# Comparative Effects of Aqueous Extract of *Moringa oleifera* Leaves and Seeds on Methamphetamine-Induced Multi-Organ Toxicity in Wistar Rats

## Project Overview
This repository contains research data and supporting materials for a PhD study investigating the comparative effects of aqueous extracts of *Moringa oleifera* leaves and seeds on methamphetamine-induced multi-organ toxicity in Wistar rats.

The study evaluates the toxicological effects of methamphetamine on multiple biochemical, inflammatory, neurochemical, reproductive, and histological parameters, and also examines the therapeutic potential of *Moringa oleifera* leaf extract (MLE) and seed extract (MSE) in comparison with standard treatment.

## Study Aim
The aim of this study is to assess the extent of methamphetamine-induced toxicity across selected organs and biochemical systems, and to compare the restorative effects of *Moringa oleifera* leaf and seed extracts.

## Data Categories
The repository currently includes or will include data related to the following categories:

- Antioxidant parameters
- Hematological parameters
- Inflammatory markers
- Kidney function parameters
- Lipid profile parameters
- Liver function parameters
- Neurotransmitters
- Reproductive hormones
- Histology scores
- GC-MS, GC-FID, and FTIR data

## Experimental Design
The study involves male and female Wistar rats grouped into normal control, methamphetamine-only groups, standard treatment groups, and treatment groups receiving aqueous leaf or seed extracts of *Moringa oleifera* at different dose levels.

Methamphetamine was administered at graded doses, and treatment effects were evaluated separately for male and female animals.

## File Organization
The repository is intended to be organized as follows:

- `data/raw/` – original uploaded datasets
- `data/cleaned/` – cleaned and analysis-ready datasets
- `data/metadata/` – data dictionary and variable descriptions
- `results/` – statistical summaries, tables, and figures
- `docs/` – notes, study documentation, and supporting materials
- `scripts/` – data cleaning, analysis, and visualization scripts

## Data Handling Note
Raw data files are preserved as original records. Cleaned files are prepared separately to support reproducibility, transparency, and downstream statistical analysis.

## Current Status
Data upload and organization are in progress. Statistical analysis, visualization, and integrative interpretation of biochemical and instrumental datasets will follow.
